The Transpose of $A$ ($A^T$) is the matrix whose columns are the rows of $A$:

$$\begin{bmatrix} 1&2&3&4 \\ 0&1&0&2  \end{bmatrix}^T=\begin{bmatrix} 1&0\\2&1\\3&0\\4&2 \end{bmatrix}$$
# Powers
$$A^k=AA\dots A$$
# Properties

1. $(A^T)^T=A$
2. $(A+B)^T=A^T+B^T$
3. $(\mathbf{r}A)^T=\mathbf{r}A^T$ 
4. $(AB)^T=B^TA^T$
